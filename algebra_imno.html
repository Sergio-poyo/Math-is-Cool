Intro to Imaginary Numbers

What are Imaginary Numbers?

Imaginary numbers are literally defined as i=sqrt(-1)

When you multiply i by another number, can you guess what happens?

If we add the exponents, we get (-1)^1, which is just 1!
Repeat this process, and we now have i*-1 = -i = i^3
Do it again at i^4, we end up with 1. i*-i = -i^2 = 1
Finally, at i^5, we just get i again! i*1 = i. Do you know why this is so?
